About

Health writing that starts with the paper, not the trend.

The Avora Life started as a place to write about sleep — the science behind why we struggle with it, and what actually helps. It's grown from there into a wider space for evidence-based health and biology writing.

It's written by a final-year biotechnology student specializing in food biotechnology, with a background spanning enzymology, microbial biotechnology, genomics, and bioinformatics. That academic lens is the whole point: every post here is built the way a research summary is built — starting from a real study, not a headline.

What this site covers

  • Sleep science and wellness, grounded in published research
  • Nutrition and its measurable effects on the body
  • Biotechnology and biology topics, explained without unnecessary jargon

What it won't do

No miracle cures, no unqualified claims, no recycled advice copied from other blogs. If a claim can't be traced to a real source, it doesn't get published here.

This site also serves as a working portfolio of science communication and biotechnology writing. If you're looking for a science writer, this is a working sample of that.
