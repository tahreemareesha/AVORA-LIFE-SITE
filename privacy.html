What happens to your data on this site.

This site may use standard analytics tools (such as Google Analytics) to understand traffic patterns — pages visited, general location, device type, and how visitors arrive at the site. This data is aggregated and is not used to personally identify visitors.

This site does not sell personal data. If a contact form, newsletter signup, or comment section is added in the future, any information submitted will only be used for the stated purpose (replying to you, or sending updates you opted into).

Cookies may be used for basic site functionality and analytics. You can disable cookies through your browser settings at any time.

If you have questions about how your data is handled on this site, you're welcome to reach out directly.
